## L2 — 강의·랩·자격증
- **Made With ML** (무료, 운영 중심) — https://madewithml.com/ · 강의는 이 하나로 제한, 80%는 손으로 짓기.
- **Full Stack Deep Learning** (무료, "학습 이후") — https://fullstackdeeplearning.com/
- **APXML VRAM Calculator** (랩) — https://apxml.com/tools/vram-calculator
- **killercoda CKA** (무료 핸즈온) — https://killercoda.com/cka
- 자격증: **CKA**(https://www.cncf.io/training/certification/cka/) + **AWS MLA-C01**(https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-machine-learning-engineer-associate/) — 이 둘로 끝.
